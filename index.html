Lil' Reaper

Welcome to Lil' Reaper. A game where you collect disturbed souls and put them to rest before they turn into Zombies. Watch out for the souls that have already transformed into Zombies!

Choose your Difficulty

Controls:

  • Move using WASD or arrow keys
  • Jump using Spacebar
  • Reap (attack Zombies and collect souls) by left-clicking or pressing R
  • Use an item by right-clicking or pressing SHIFT
